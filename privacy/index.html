Food Count – Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 05, 2025

1. We collect NO data

Food Count does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. Period.

All inventory counts, par levels, categories, and settings are saved only on your device using Apple’s local UserDefaults storage. Nothing ever leaves your iPhone or iPad.

2. No tracking, no analytics, no ads

  • No analytics tools (no Firebase, no Mixpanel, no nothing)
  • No crash reporters
  • No advertising SDKs
  • No cookies, no pixels, no fingerprints

3. Permissions? Zero.

Food Count does not request access to:

  • Camera
  • Photos
  • Location
  • Microphone
  • Contacts
  • Or anything else

4. App Store privacy label

You’ll see this exact label on the App Store:

“Data Not Collected”
The developer does not collect any data from this app.
